Neck rolls
5 each side · 45 s
▼
🎾 Releases ball-watching tension
Sit or stand tall. Slowly drop your right ear toward your right shoulder. Roll your chin gently down toward your chest, then up to the left. Keep it slow — no fast circles. Breathe out on each drop.

Phase 2 — Spine & hips 5 min
🐱
Cat-cow
10 slow reps · 60 s
▼
🎾 Bend-and-reach during rallies
Hands under shoulders, knees under hips. Inhale as you drop your belly and lift your tailbone (cow). Exhale as you round your back to the ceiling and tuck your chin (cat). Move with your breath, hold each end 2 s.

▼
🎾 Pivoting & split-step recovery
Sit on the floor, both knees bent at 90° — one in front, one to the side. Gently lean toward the front knee, then rotate your hips to switch sides. Use your hands for support. Work to your comfortable edge only.

▼
🎾 Wide ball retrieval & court coverage

▼
🎾 Unlocks serve power
Stand in a doorway, forearms on the frame at 90°. Gently lean forward until you feel a stretch across chest and front of shoulders. Hold 20 s. Keep shoulders down — don't shrug.
